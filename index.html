How PPSU Attendance System Works

At PPSU, attendance is calculated lecture-by-lecture, not by days. Each subject has its own attendance tracking system.

  • Attendance is marked in every single lecture (yes, every one!)
  • Minimum requirement is 75% attendance to be eligible for exams
  • This calculator helps you plan your bunk strategy wisely!
  • Set your target and see how many lectures you can afford to miss
  • Track your progress throughout the semester

Pro Tip: Gain high attendance early in the semester by attending all initial lectures. This creates a buffer that allows you to bunk more lectures later without falling below the required percentage!
