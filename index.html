FAQs (From Panicking Students, For Panicking Students)

What is the PPSU attendance 75 rule? +
PPSU says: attend at least 75% of lectures per subject or you can't sit in the exam. No negotiation, no discount, no "but sir my dog was sick." This PPSU attendance calculator helps you stay on the right side of 75%.
How many lectures can I bunk at PPSU? +
Roughly 1 out of every 4 lectures — but the exact number depends on YOUR current attendance. Just use the PPSU bunk calculator above. That's literally why this page exists.
What happens if I go below 75% attendance at PPSU? +
Short version: you get detained (can't appear for exams). Long version: parents get a letter, your semester becomes a very expensive holiday, and you may have to repeat. Avoid. Just attend.
Are cancelled / holiday lectures counted in PPSU attendance? +
No! Cancelled or no-attendance days are excluded from the total. Enter them in the "Cancelled Lectures" field and this PPSU attendance 75 calculator handles the rest automatically.
Can I set a custom target like 78% or 82%? +
Yes! Drag the slider to any number between 50% and 100%. If it's not one of the presets (65, 75, 85, 90), the button shows "Custom %" — because you're special. 💙
